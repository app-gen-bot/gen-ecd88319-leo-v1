# AI Tenant Rights Advisor - California Edition

Create a comprehensive web application that serves as an AI-powered legal advisor for landlord-tenant disputes in California. The app should help both tenants and landlords navigate their rights and responsibilities while providing tools to prevent and resolve disputes.

## Core Features

### 1. Smart Move-In/Move-Out Documentation
- **Video Walk-Through Analysis**: Users can upload or record video walk-throughs of the property
- **AI Damage Detection**: Computer vision analyzes videos/photos to identify and catalog existing damage, wear, or issues
- **Timestamped Evidence**: Automatically timestamps and stores all documentation with blockchain-like verification
- **Comparison Reports**: Side-by-side analysis of move-in vs move-out conditions
- **Damage Cost Estimator**: AI estimates repair costs based on California standards

### 2. AI Legal Advisor Chat
- **Context-Aware Assistance**: Chatbot trained on California tenant law (Civil Code 1940-1954.1)
- **Situation Analysis**: Users describe their issue and get specific legal guidance
- **Document Review**: Upload lease agreements for AI analysis and red flag detection
- **Rights Explanation**: Plain English explanations of complex legal rights
- **Action Plan Generator**: Step-by-step guidance for resolving disputes

### 3. Smart Lease Management
- **Lease Template Generator**: California-compliant lease agreements with customizable clauses
- **Lease Review AI**: Analyzes uploaded leases for illegal clauses or missing required disclosures
- **Amendment Tracker**: Manage lease modifications with proper documentation
- **Renewal Reminders**: Automated notifications for lease renewals and required notices

### 4. Dispute Resolution Center
- **Dispute Wizard**: Guided process to document and categorize disputes
- **Evidence Collection**: Organize photos, videos, emails, texts in one place
- **Timeline Builder**: Visual timeline of events with legal significance highlighted
- **Letter Generator**: AI creates formal letters (repair requests, notices, demand letters)
- **Small Claims Helper**: Forms and filing guidance for California small claims court

### 5. Financial Tracking
- **Security Deposit Calculator**: Track deposits with interest calculations per CA law
- **Rent Payment Logger**: Document all payments with receipt generation
- **Expense Tracker**: Track repair costs, improvements, and deductible expenses
- **Late Fee Validator**: Ensures late fees comply with lease and CA law

### 6. Communication Hub
- **Message Center**: Secure communication between landlords and tenants
- **Read Receipts**: Legally admissible proof of notice delivery
- **Template Library**: Pre-written messages for common situations
- **Translation Support**: Multi-language support for diverse California population

### 7. Maintenance Management
- **Request Portal**: Submit and track maintenance requests
- **urgency Classifier**: AI determines if issue is emergency, urgent, or routine
- **Contractor Network**: Connect with verified local contractors
- **Inspection Scheduler**: Coordinate property inspections with notifications

### 8. Legal Document Library
- **Form Repository**: All California-required forms (3-day notices, habitability notices, etc.)
- **Auto-Fill Forms**: Pre-populate forms with saved information
- **E-Signature Integration**: Legally binding electronic signatures
- **Document Storage**: Secure cloud storage with encryption

### 9. Tenant Screening (For Landlords)
- **Background Check Integration**: Criminal, credit, and eviction history
- **Income Verification**: Automated income and employment verification
- **Reference Checker**: AI-powered reference verification system
- **Fair Housing Compliance**: Ensures screening criteria comply with fair housing laws

### 10. Knowledge Base
- **California Law Library**: Searchable database of relevant laws and regulations
- **Case Law Examples**: Real-world examples and precedents
- **City-Specific Rules**: Local ordinances for major CA cities (LA, SF, SD, etc.)
- **Video Tutorials**: How-to guides for common procedures

## User Types
1. **Tenants**: Full access to tenant rights features, dispute tools, and legal guidance
2. **Landlords**: Property management tools, screening, and compliance features
3. **Property Managers**: Multi-property support with team collaboration

## Key California-Specific Features
- Rent control calculator for applicable cities
- Just cause eviction validator
- COVID-19 tenant protection tracker
- Seismic retrofit disclosure management
- Bedbug disclosure handling
- Prop 65 warning compliance

## Technology Requirements
- Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go property documentation
- AI/ML for image analysis and natural language processing
- Secure document storage with encryption
- Integration with e-signature services
- Real-time notification system
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese)

## Monetization
- Freemium model: Basic features free, premium for advanced AI analysis
- Per-document pricing for official letters and forms
- Subscription tiers for landlords based on number of properties
- Referral fees from contractor network
- Premium dispute resolution services

Build this as a modern, user-friendly web application that empowers both tenants and landlords to handle their legal matters confidently while reducing the need for expensive legal consultations.
