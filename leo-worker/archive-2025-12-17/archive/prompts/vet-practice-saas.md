# PawsFlow - Veterinary Practice Management SaaS

Build a comprehensive cloud-based veterinary practice management system called **PawsFlow** that streamlines clinic operations, automates billing, ensures compliance, and improves patient care coordination. The name reflects the smooth flow of operations and the focus on paw-some patient care.

## Core Features

### Appointment & Schedule Management
- Multi-doctor, multi-room scheduling with drag-and-drop interface
- Automated appointment reminders via SMS/email
- Online booking portal for pet owners
- Recurring appointment scheduling for vaccines/checkups
- Waitlist management and smart scheduling optimization

### Patient & Medical Records
- Complete electronic health records (EHR) for pets
- Photo/video attachment for documenting conditions
- Vaccination tracking with automated reminders
- Lab results integration and tracking
- Treatment plan templates for common conditions
- SOAP notes with voice-to-text capability

### Billing & Invoicing
- Automated invoice generation from treatments
- Insurance claim processing and tracking
- Payment processing integration (credit cards, ACH)
- Payment plans and financing options
- Inventory-based automatic pricing updates
- Financial reporting and analytics

### Inventory Management
- Real-time inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
- Automated reordering based on usage patterns
- Drug interaction warnings and dosage calculators
- Controlled substance compliance tracking
- Vendor management and price comparison

### Client Communication
- Two-way messaging portal with clients
- Automated follow-up messages post-visit
- Pet health education content delivery
- Prescription refill requests
- Emergency contact management

### Compliance & Reporting
- AAHA accreditation compliance tools
- State veterinary board reporting
- Controlled substance logging (DEA compliance)
- Rabies registry integration
- Quality assurance metrics tracking

### Team Collaboration
- Shift scheduling and time tracking
- Internal messaging and task management
- Performance dashboards for staff
- Continuing education tracking
- Role-based access control

### Business Intelligence
- Practice analytics dashboard
- Revenue cycle management
- Client retention metrics
- Treatment outcome tracking
- Competitive benchmarking tools

## Technical Requirements
- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- HIPAA-compliant data security
- Mobile-responsive design
- Offline mode for critical functions
- Integration with common vet equipment
- API for third-party integrations

## User Personas
1. **Veterinarians**: Need efficient patient care workflow and medical decision support
2. **Vet Technicians**: Require easy documentation and task management
3. **Front Desk Staff**: Need appointment and billing tools
4. **Practice Managers**: Want analytics and compliance oversight
5. **Pet Owners**: Desire convenient booking and communication

## Success Metrics
- Reduce appointment scheduling time by 70%
- Decrease billing errors by 90%
- Improve client communication response time by 80%
- Increase practice revenue by 25% through optimization
- Achieve 95%+ user satisfaction rating

## Market Differentiation
Focus on integrated workflow automation that connects all aspects of practice management, not just individual features. Emphasize mobile-first design for vets on the go and AI-powered insights for practice optimization.
